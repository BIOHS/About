What is BIOHS website ?

BIOHS is a website that provides comprehensive summaries for the units of health science subjects for grades 11 and 12.
In term 3 biology comprehensive summaries will be available in the website for G11 advanced & G12 general.

What is its purpose?

Its purpose is to communicate ideas in simple and organized ways, to consolidate ideas in smooth ways, as in our slogan: "Seeing things simpler makes work easier".
